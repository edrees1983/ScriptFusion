ScriptFusion

3D in Scratch is hard but possible. To get started i recommend to use Griffpatch's 3D maze tutorials, if you're interested in making a 3D platformer or game, voxelbear is the easiest to learn from in his tutorials, if you want to make Minecraft in Scratch, use FindingPeppers tutorials. After you gathered some skills try to make your own 3D game!
